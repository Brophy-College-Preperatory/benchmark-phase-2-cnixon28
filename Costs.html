Costs

This is the biggest factor when getting a car, and everyone has their own budget for the car. A not-as-expensive car such as a Toyota Corolla will get the job done but won't look as nice, be as comfortable, or drive as well as a more expensive car. If you are getting your first car, you typically start with a non-expensive car and get a more expensive one later in your life when you can afford it. All of the cars in my list of the best-looking cars were expensive, showing that a good-looking car will almost always be expensive.

Not Expensive Car

  • Won't look as nice
  • Won't drive as well
  • Less comfortable
  • More likely to break

Expensive Car

  • Looks better
  • Drives better
  • More comfortable
  • More reliable
